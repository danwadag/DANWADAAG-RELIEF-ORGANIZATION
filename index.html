Danwadaag Relief Organization

A dedicated local humanitarian non-profit working to uplift vulnerabilities, provide emergencies response, and build sustainable solutions in Southwest State, Somalia.

Who We Are

Initiated in November 2023 by a group of intellectuals and well-wishers, DRO focuses on aspects of emergencies, humanitarian responses, and resilience programs in close collaboration with UN agencies and international organizations.

Our Mission

To eradicate poverty, provide quality social services, health, and water to promote high standard living of IDPs and vulnerabilities to achieve sustainable solutions for all.

Our Vision

Upgrading living standards of the vulnerable groups and young generations by mobilizing their conscious awareness on ownership feelings transforming into self-reliance.

Core Values

  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Volunteerism & Social Responsibility
  • Respect for Diversity
  • Collaboration

Guiding Principles

  • Independence
  • Neutrality
  • Impartiality & Humanity

Core Programs

Primary Health Care & Nutrition

Improving access to healthcare services for IDPs and host communities through mobile clinics, alongside managing and preventing malnutrition among children under five and mothers.

WASH Services

Supplying safe water, providing gender-segregated latrines, conducting active disease surveillance, and managing solid waste to prevent disease outbreaks in communities.

Child Protection & Education

Strengthening family/community structures, raising public awareness on child rights, and supporting child-centered training to safeguard vulnerable children.

Agriculture & Poverty Reduction

Ensuring food security and improving the livelihoods of small farmers on a sustainable basis while working on strategic frameworks to alleviate rural poverty.

IDPs & Protection Support

Mitigating the root causes of internal displacement, protecting fundamental human rights, and building resilient frameworks to help communities recover.

SGBV Prevention

Reinforcing institutional support services available to victims, advocating against gender-based violence, and driving long-term positive behavior change.
