Who We Are

Danwadaag Relief Organization (DRO) is an established local non-profit, non-governmental humanitarian entity headquartered in Baidoa, Somalia, with operational presence stretching across the Southwest State.

Initiated in November 2023 by a dedicated group of intellectuals and well-wishers, DRO functions in direct coordination with community structures, UN agencies, and international bodies to counter devastating natural disasters and recurring health crises.

Our Core Programmatic Areas

DRO scales specialized, targeted micro-and-macro operations inside complex vulnerable zones through nine integrated sectors.

1. Primary Health & Nutrition

Deploying emergency mobile health clinics to remote IDP settlements and scaling direct nutritional tracking for vulnerable mothers and under-five infants.

2. Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)
