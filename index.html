Mandate & Philosophy

Formed by an integrated coalition of regional intellectuals and well-wishers to support communities facing sudden environmental or structural disruptions.

Our Core Mission & Vision

Mission: To eradicate poverty and provide quality social services. Promoting high-quality living standards of IDPs and vulnerable groups to achieve sustainable solutions for all.

Vision: Upgrading living standards of the vulnerable groups and young generations by mobilizing conscious awareness on ownership feelings transforming into self-reliance.

"The true development of individuality assumes a functional meaning only within a healthy social environment. Therefore, let operational programs burn the individual flame, feeding it continuously with the protective oil of society."
