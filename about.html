Organization Overview

[span_31](start_span)

Danwadaag Relief Organization (DRO) is an established local non-profit, non-governmental humanitarian organization headquartered within Baidoa town, maintaining active operational outreach across the regions of Somalia[span_31](end_span). [span_32](start_span)The framework was initiated in November 2023 by an unified coalition of regional intellectuals and well-wishers[span_32](end_span).

[span_33](start_span)

DRO directs targeted operations across Bay, Bakool, and Lower Shebelle[span_33](end_span). [span_34](start_span)Operating as a dedicated Community Based Organization, DRO executes baseline field relief in close alignment and collaboration with UN Agencies and international partner groups to protect families facing sudden climate, environmental, or conflict disruptions[span_34](end_span).

Operational Goal & Synthesis Philosophy

[span_35](start_span)

Our long-term operational objective focuses on upgrading living standards for vulnerable groups and young generations by shifting conscious community attention towards ownership and self-reliance[span_35](end_span).
