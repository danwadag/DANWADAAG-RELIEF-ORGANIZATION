Institutional Background

[span_11](start_span)

Danwadaag Relief Organization (DRO) is a local non-profit, non-governmental organization headquartered in Baidoa town, with active sub-offices across the regions of Somalia[span_11](end_span). [span_12](start_span)Initiated in November 2023 by a dedicated group of intellectuals and well-wishers, DRO answers critical calls for human assistance and community development[span_12](end_span).

[span_13](start_span)

DRO operates as a specialized community-based group working for humanitarian relief with close operational collaboration with UN Agencies and International organizations[span_13](end_span). [span_14](start_span)Our focus fields structurally counter the vulnerabilities affecting regional populations impacted by severe natural disasters and sudden disease outbreaks[span_14](end_span).

Our Strategic Mission

[span_15](start_span)

To eradicate poverty, provide quality social services, health, and clean water while promoting a high standard of living for IDPs and vulnerable community cohorts to achieve sustainable solutions for all[span_15](end_span).

Our Vision Matrix

[span_16](start_span)

Upgrading the living standards of the vulnerable groups and young generations by mobilizing their conscious awareness on ownership feelings, effectively transforming dependence into sustainable self-reliance[span_16](end_span).
