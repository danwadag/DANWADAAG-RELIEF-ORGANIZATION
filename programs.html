SECTOR 1

Primary Health Care & Nutrition

1. Primary Health Care Program Objective

[span_2](start_span)

To directly systematically improve the broad availability of, and immediate field access to, essential healthcare service components for internally displaced populations (IDPs) and marginalized host community networks, placing vital priority focus onto highly vulnerable clusters like children utilizing highly agile mobile medical clinics as well as localized healthcare promotion architectures[span_2](end_span).

2. Management and Prevention of Malnutrition

[span_3](start_span)

[span_4](start_span)Our direct healthcare interventions target systemic disease overlays such as malaria, measles, and seasonal diarrhea, which are intensely prevalent and rapidly precipitate child wasting in camps[span_4](end_span). SECTOR 3

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

[span_13](start_span)

For IDP communities displaced by shifting security environments, direct structural access to completely secure clean water lines and functional sanitization frameworks is an urgent life priority[span_13](end_span). [span_14](start_span)Without safe drinking water and systematic hygiene promotion, fighting off communicable illness becomes statistically impossible[span_14](end_span).

Operational Focus Pillars

    [span_15](start_span)
  • Safe Water Logistics: Ongoing supply of tested, treated water for drinking, cooking frameworks, personal care, and systemic home sanitization[span_15](end_span).
    • [span_16](start_span)
  • Gender-Segregated Infrastructure: Developing and maintaining family or sex-segregated latrine infrastructures that assure safe, dignified access for women and girls at all hours[span_16](end_span).
    • [span_17](start_span)
  • Surveillance & Outbreak Blockades: Orchestrating hyper-vigilant epidemiological disease surveillance on local water lines to intercept diarrheal spikes during active environmental emergencies[span_17](end_span).
    • [span_18](start_span)
  • Solid Waste Logistics: Engineering site drainage channels to permanently flush out standing open water matrices, preventing vector breeding fields like mosquitoes and vermin[span_18](end_span).
